# Vyira Brand Outreach

This document keeps Vyira's early brand publishing consistent, ethical, and easy to track.

## Important rules

- Do not spam Quora, Reddit, or forums with promotional posts.
- Do not pretend to be an unrelated customer or third party.
- Do not create a Wikipedia page unless Vyira has strong independent coverage from reliable publications.
- Prefer useful, honest, story-led content over direct promotion.

## Core brand line

`Elegance by nature`

## Short brand story

Vyira is a modern apparel brand inspired by Telugu Padmashali roots, where fabric is respected for its care, craft, and feel. The brand focuses on clean design, soft comfort, and thoughtful materials so clothing feels right from the first wear.

## Founder story

After 15 years in IT, founder Vinay Vemula chose to build something closer to home. Vyira brings together a family respect for fabric, a modern design sensibility, and a simple goal: clothing that feels refined, comfortable, and effortless.

## Fabric message

Vyira focuses on materials that support comfort and finish, including bamboo, hemp, linen, Tencel, and organic cotton. The positioning is not loud luxury. It is thoughtful design, premium finish, and great feel.

## Platform approach

### Quora

Use Quora only when answering real questions such as:

- What are good sustainable fabric choices for clothing?
- Why does fabric feel matter so much in premium apparel?
- What makes bamboo or Tencel comfortable to wear?
- How can a new fashion brand stand out without copying fast fashion?

Style:

- answer the question first
- mention Vyira briefly and naturally only where relevant
- avoid repetitive brand drops

Example soft mention:

"That idea is also shaping how we think about Vyira, where the focus is on clean design and fabrics that feel good from the first wear."

### Reddit

Use Reddit carefully. Most subreddits reject self-promotion.

Safer angles:

- join discussions about fabrics, clothing feel, sustainability, and design
- share useful knowledge, not ads
- only mention Vyira if the subreddit rules allow it and the mention is directly relevant

Avoid:

- posting the same brand story across many subreddits
- pretending to be an independent fan
- dropping links without context

### Medium or LinkedIn articles

These are better early channels for founder-led storytelling.

Good article topics:

- Why fabric feel matters more than logos
- What Telugu Padmashali heritage taught us about clothing
- Why modern apparel should feel effortless, not overdesigned
- Sustainable fabrics we believe in: bamboo, hemp, linen, Tencel, and organic cotton

## Wikipedia reality check

Vyira should not have a Wikipedia page yet unless it has:

- multiple independent articles about the brand
- reliable secondary coverage
- clear notability beyond the brand's own website and social media

Better first step:

- build independent press mentions
- publish founder interviews
- get coverage from fashion, startup, design, or sustainability publications

## Recommended publishing order

1. Publish 2-3 strong founder or fabric articles on LinkedIn or Medium.
2. Reach out to niche fashion, textile, sustainability, and startup publications.
3. Participate helpfully on Quora and Reddit without spamming.
4. Track every mention in `brand-mentions.csv`.
5. Revisit Wikipedia only after independent coverage exists.
