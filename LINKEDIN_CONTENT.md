## Post 3: Fabric focus

### Caption

At Vyira, fabric comes first.

We are especially drawn to materials like bamboo, hemp, linen, Tencel, and organic cotton for one reason:

they help clothing feel better.

Softer on the skin.
Lighter in wear.
Cleaner in finish.

For us, design is not only about how a garment looks.
It is also about how naturally it moves, rests, and feels through the day.

That is the direction behind Vyira.

Elegance by nature.

#Vyira #Fabric #BambooFabric #Linen #Tencel #OrganicCotton #SustainableFashion
